Pioneer Day

Pioneer Day is a day where we celebrate our Pioneer heritage and have a big parade. Come to the Field Park located behind the city center to watch the parade and participate in activities and look at booths afterwards. I am sure you will have lots of fun. No shoot and soak though, because our city has lost its sense of childlike wonder and joy.
