Membership Information

Non-Profit Membership

Non-Profit Organization receive free membership! Non-Profit Patrons receive charity status, giving access to events and advertising.

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership costs $50 per year. Bronze Patrons receive our weekly newsletter and access to certain events.

Silver Membership

The Silver Membership costs $100 per year. Silver Patrons have rights to parades other exclusive events. Plus, Silver Membership gives access to all Bronze level benefits.

Gold Membership

The Gold Membership costs $200 per year. Gold patrons are prominently featured on our website and are given access to the best advertising slots and booth locations at all events. Gold Patrons also receive all of the Silver and Bronze level benefits.
