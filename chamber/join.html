Membership Info

Non Profit Membership

The Non Profit Membership is free! It entitles you to charity status. Access to events and advertising.

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership costs $50 per year. Bronze Patrons are allowed in our weekly newsletter and access to certain events.

Silver Membership

The Silver Membership costs $100 per year. Silver Patrons are exclusive right to parades and more exclusive events. Plus all previous perks aside from the Non Profit perks.

Gold Membership

The Gold Memebership costs $200 per year. Gold patrons are prominently featured on our website and are given access to the best advertising slots, and booth locations at all events. Gold Patrons also have access to prevous perks aside from Non Profit perks.
