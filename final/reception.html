Reception Hall Accomodations

  • The reception hall is 70 yards by 45 yards.
  • Themed decorations are available.
  • Patrons can bring their own decorations, if they wish.
  • The receptions hall can hold up to 250 people.
  • Tables and chairs are provided.

Food Catering

  • There are three basic menus based around the following international cuisines: Japan, Mexico, Carribean.
  • When scheduling receptions you can look at the menus and choose which items to add or subtract.
  • Reception patrons are allowed to bring or create their own food in the provided kitchen.

Hall and Food Set up

  • All set up and take down will be done by our staff.
  • Finish planning three days prior to the reception in order for our staff to have adequate preparation.
  • Reception menu will must be finalized a week prior to the reception.
